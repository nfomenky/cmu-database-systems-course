Chapters 3-5 Advanced SQL.

Aggregates:
-AVG, MIN, MAX, SUM, COUNT.

String operations:
-case sensitive
-UPPER(s), LOWER(s)
-LIKE: use with '%' and '_'
-Substrings: SUBSTRING(s,0,n)
-Concatenate: s || s

Nested Queries are often difficult to optimize.
-ALL, ANY, IN, NOT, EXISTS
- IN is eq. to '= ANY()'
 

-Window functions perform calculation across a set of of tuples

- Common table expressions provide an alternative to window functions or nested queries.
